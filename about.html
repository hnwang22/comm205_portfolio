I was born in Beijing, China, where I grew up before immigrating to the United States (more specifically, Texas). I was raised not far from Tsinghua University, where my grandfather taught as a professor (and where my father obtained his engineering degree).

After moving to Texas, my family moved around for a bit, before I graduated high school and decided to attend Texas A&M University for Electrical Engineering (I would later switch to Computer Engineering).
